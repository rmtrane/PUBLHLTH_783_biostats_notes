It can be calculated as \({n \choose k} = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}\), where \(n!\) (“\(n\) factorial”) is \(n\cdot (n-1) \cdot (n-2) \cdot ... \cdot 2 \cdot 1\). By definition, \(0! = 1\).↩︎
